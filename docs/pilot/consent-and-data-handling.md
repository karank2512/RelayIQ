# Consent & Data Handling — Pilot Interviews and Demos

Plain-English policy for anyone participating in a RelayIQ interview, audit, or demo.
Read this aloud (or link it) at the start of every session.

> **Not legal advice.** This document describes how I personally handle interview
> information. It is not a legal contract, a DPA, or compliance advice, and I am not a
> lawyer. If your organization needs formal terms before talking, we should skip the
> interview rather than improvise them.

## What is collected

- **Interview notes.** Written notes about your workflow, tools, spend ranges, and pain
  points. That's the entire intended dataset.
- **Recordings — only if you say yes.** Sessions are not recorded by default. If you
  agree to a recording, it's used only to correct my notes and is deleted after the notes
  are finalized.
- **Contact details.** Your name, role, and email, used only to schedule and to send you
  anything I promised (summary, demo link).

## What is *not* collected

- No production data. I will never ask for CRM exports, contact lists, provider API keys,
  credentials, or access to your systems.
- No screen-sharing of your live systems is required. If you choose to show something,
  I'll note what I learned but won't capture screenshots or recordings of it.
- Nothing you say is entered into the RelayIQ system itself. The product database contains
  only synthetic records.

## The demo is synthetic-data-only

Every demo runs against a generated dataset: fictional companies and people using
RFC-2606-reserved `.test` domains, served by deterministic provider *simulators* with a
known ground truth. Nothing in the demo is, or is derived from, real-world personal data —
yours or anyone else's. There is nothing for you to connect, install, or upload.

## How your information is used

- Notes are used to decide what to build and to write anonymized, aggregated findings
  (e.g., "several interviewees reported re-enriching the same records").
- **Quotes:** never attributed — by name, company, or identifying description — without
  your explicit written approval of the exact quote and attribution.
- Notes are not sold, shared with third parties, or used for marketing lists.

## Deletion on request

Email me at any time and I will delete your interview notes, any recording, and your
contact details, and confirm when it's done. No reason needed, no questions asked.
Aggregated statements that no longer identify you (e.g., a count of interviews) may
remain.

## Questions

Ask before, during, or after the session — and if any answer here isn't acceptable to
you, we simply won't do that part.
