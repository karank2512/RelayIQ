# Pilot Intake Form — Questions

Question set for the pilot intake form (Google Forms / Tally / Typeform — any tool).
Purpose: qualify and segment interviewees before the 20-minute call, and capture
comparable baseline data across pilots.

Form intro text (paste verbatim):

> Thanks for your interest. This takes about 3 minutes. Answers are used only to prepare
> for our conversation and for anonymized, aggregated research. No production data is
> requested here or at any later point — the RelayIQ demo runs entirely on synthetic data.
> You can request deletion of your answers at any time.

---

## Section 1 — About you

1. Name *(short text)*
2. Work email *(short text)*
3. Role *(single choice)*
   - RevOps / Marketing Ops
   - GTM / Growth engineer
   - Agency founder or operator
   - Sales / SDR leadership
   - Data / Engineering
   - Other: ___
4. Company size *(single choice)*: 1–10 / 11–50 / 51–200 / 201–1000 / 1000+
5. How many people touch your enrichment workflow (build it, run it, review its output)?
   *(single choice)*: just me / 2–3 / 4–10 / more than 10

## Section 2 — Stack

6. Which tools are part of your enrichment workflow today? *(multi-select)*
   - Clay
   - HubSpot
   - Salesforce
   - Spreadsheets / CSV pipelines
   - Homegrown scripts or internal service
   - Zapier / Make / n8n
   - Other: ___
7. Which data providers do you buy from? *(multi-select)*
   - Apollo
   - ZoomInfo
   - Clearbit / HubSpot Breeze
   - People Data Labs
   - Prospeo / Findymail / email-finder tools
   - Clay-internal waterfall providers
   - Other: ___
8. Do you use provider waterfalls (try provider A, fall back to B)? *(single choice)*
   yes, in a tool / yes, built ourselves / no / not sure

## Section 3 — Spend

9. Roughly what do you spend on enrichment per month, all providers combined?
   *(single choice)*
   - Under $250
   - $250–$1,000
   - $1,000–$5,000
   - $5,000–$25,000
   - Over $25,000
   - Prefer not to say
10. Is enrichment spend budgeted per campaign or per client? *(single choice)*
    yes / no / sort of (explain): ___
11. Have you ever measured how much of that spend was wasted (duplicates, stale data,
    unusable records)? *(single choice)* measured it / estimated it / never tried

## Section 4 — Pain ranking

12. Rank these from most painful (1) to least painful (6) in your workflow: *(ranking)*
    - Paying to re-enrich records we already had
    - Stale data (titles/companies out of date)
    - Providers disagreeing with each other
    - Bad data reaching the CRM / sales team
    - No visibility into where enrichment spend goes
    - Manual review and cleanup effort
13. Anything painful that isn't on that list? *(long text, optional)*

## Section 5 — Logistics

14. Are you up for a 20-minute interview about your workflow? *(single choice)*
    yes / async written answers only / no, just curious
15. Anything you want to make sure we cover? *(long text, optional)*
16. Consent check *(must accept to submit)*:
    "I've read the consent and data-handling summary (link `consent-and-data-handling.md`)
    and understand that no production data is requested and I can ask for deletion at any
    time."

---

## Handling submissions

- Store responses wherever the form tool puts them; export to the pilot's
  `findings-template.md` copy when an interview is scheduled.
- Spend and waste answers are **reported** figures — label them as such everywhere.
- Deletion requests: purge the form response, exports, and any notes; confirm by email.
